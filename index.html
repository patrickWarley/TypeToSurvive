This is a game based on the "The Typing of the Dead" made as a exercise in javascript, css, and html5!

The challenge is to get to highest level possible.
You will level up after a number of hits.
A hit is a word typed right inside the time limit.
Each level increase the number of letters in the words by 1 starting from 3.
Good luck!
